WHAT THIS IMPLEMENTATION DOES
================================================================================

Reverse Stress Testing (RST) is a method to identify supply chain 
vulnerabilities by working BACKWARDS from a disruption outcome to find 
the most likely causes.

Traditional Approach:        Reverse Stress Testing:
"What if X happens?"    â†’    "What could cause Y?"
Scenario-based          â†’    Threat-agnostic
Limited coverage        â†’    Comprehensive analysis

Example: "What could cause a 20% drop in US copper wire imports?"
â†’ RST identifies: Canada (refined copper), Chile (unrefined copper), 
  Turkey (ore), Brazil (ore), etc. with probabilities
